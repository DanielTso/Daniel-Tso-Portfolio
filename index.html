About Me

From the Field to the Future of Project Management

I'm not your typical construction project manager. With 33 years of experience spanning the complete project lifecycle — from hands-on trades to engineering design to construction management — I bring a perspective most professionals simply don't have. I started learning construction working summers with my father, uncle, and brothers in the family business, mastering welding, rigging, ironworking, and heavy equipment operation. After earning my BS in Construction Management, I worked as a CADD Designer and Project Designer at engineering firms, then spent 11 years with Bechtel Corporation progressing from Field Engineer to Construction Manager on projects up to $60 million. This unique journey means I understand trades work, design intent, AND field constructability — a rare combination that makes me exceptionally effective at coordinating complex projects.

My career has taken me across the United States managing diverse projects in mining, refineries, manufacturing, power plants, and now mission-critical data centers. I've stepped up in crisis moments — taking over troubled projects, managing during strikes, and delivering results when the pressure is highest. Currently, I serve as QA BIM Coordinator for Dilling Group Inc on a mission-critical Google Datacenter project, proving I can integrate design knowledge, field expertise, and modern technical coordination seamlessly. With continuous training in BIM, ProCore, SAP, AI integration, and emerging construction technologies, I represent the perfect combination: old-school work ethic and field expertise married with new-school technology and coordination skills.
